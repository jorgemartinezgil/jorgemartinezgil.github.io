About Dr. Jorge Martinez-Gil

I am Dr. Jorge Martinez-Gil, a computer scientist with a passion for Data and Knowledge Engineering. My journey in research began with a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Malaga, Spain, in 2010.

With a career spanning across Austria, Germany, and Spain, I've embraced various research roles, notably at the Software Competence Center Hagenberg (SCCH) in Austria. My experience includes an acting professorship at the University of Extremadura, Spain.

As an educator, I've delved into subjects like Big Data and Cloud Computing at the University of Applied Sciences Upper Austria. My commitment to teaching is evident from my involvement in supervising numerous theses and my teaching stints across the European Union.

My academic contributions include over 70 scientific publications in esteemed journals and participation in numerous conferences. As an Associate Editor of Expert Systems with Applications and a prolific reviewer, I play an active role in the scholarly community.

My work has been recognized with awards, including the Best Academic Performance from the University of Extremadura and a DAAD Honorific Scholarship. I am driven by a quest for knowledge and a desire to contribute to the field of computer science.
