About Dr. Jorge Martinez-Gil

Dr. Jorge Martinez-Gil is a computer scientist specializing in Data and Knowledge Engineering. He earned his Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Malaga in Spain in 2010. Dr. Martinez-Gil has held various research positions in countries such as Austria, Germany, and Spain, including a current position at the Software Competence Center Hagenberg (SCCH) in Austria. He previously served as an acting professor at the University of Extremadura in Spain.

In addition to his research, Dr. Martinez-Gil is actively involved in teaching, including subjects like Big Data and Cloud Computing at the University of Applied Sciences Upper Austria in Hagenberg. He has supervised numerous Bachelor's and Master's theses and has extensive teaching experience in universities across the European Union.

Dr. Martinez-Gil has a publication record with over 70 scientific publications, including numerous journal papers in prestigious journals like Knowledge and Information Systems, Information Systems Frontiers, and Knowledge-Based Systems. He has also contributed to various conferences.

His service to the academic community includes evaluating scientific manuscripts, project proposals, and study programs for over 25 funding agencies across Europe. He ranks second in WoS-Austria for Computer Science review activities with over 330 reviews for top academic journals. Additionally, he serves as an Associate Editor of Expert Systems with Applications.

Dr. Martinez-Gil has received several awards, including the Award for Best Academic Performance from the University of Extremadura in 2007 and an Honorific Scholarship from the German DAAD in 2009.
